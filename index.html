Event info & Schedule

Welcome all to the 1st edition of the indie TTRPG bonanza that we call DEATH BY DIE FEST 2024!


On the 5th of October you can join us at Annelundsgården in Malmö, southern Sweden for a day full of TTRPG madness and kitbashing!

We are running the first ever World Championships of MiniGangs!
A very beginner friendly game written by Curtis Fell at Ramshackle Games!
But don’t be fooled - it can also be very treacherous even for the more
experienced players!
But all in all: just tons of fun!
To participate in this tournament you can either bring your own 4 custom minis
- or you can borrow a gang that we built and painted for the event!
The rules are literally only two pages and can be downloaded here:
ramshacklegames.co.uk

We play with the gateway rules, but with some modifications for tournament play
- but we’ll explain all this when we meet at the fest!
There’s 27 places played over 3 tables and 3 heats for the tournament, plus the finals
And you’ll need to sign up on the form below, plus give us 5€ of you hard earned money!
(Don’t worry - they’ll be invested straight into the production of some epic boards!
This amount you will be able to pay on the day! But please consider your application as binding nevertheless…)

“OTHER GAMES? YES OF COURSE!”
There will also be a number of other games set up for everyone to play! Forbidden Psalm and The Last War - to name a few! Join our discord server and talk to other players and set up some battles here:
discord.gg/fbdbdVAhw7

The makers of Europas Ruiner and Rost o Rota will set up and demonstrate their games during the day - and will be able to let you dive in for a minute, or hours!
NOT BASHING SKULLS ON THE BATTLEFIELD? BASH PLASTIC INSTEAD!
And of course - there shall be kitbashing! Free for all! And you can even enter the competition - where Veronica Levin (@florescenta.grotesque) will decide who’s the most crazy of you all!

WE WILL ENTERTAIN YOU!
To finish it all if there will be some vendors, live music and DJ’s! We’ll announce the artists a bit later on - but let’s just say we have some goodies for you…
If you have any questions at all - feel free to ask away in our discord server!
Hope to see you all on the 5th of October in Malmö!
/ Scania Alternative Wargamers
