Event info & Schedule

Welcome all to the 1st edition of the indie TTRPG bonanza that we call DEATH BY DIE FEST 2024!


On the 5th of October you can join us at PlanB in Malmö, southern Sweden for a day full of TTRPG madness and kitbashing!

MINIGANGS WORLD CHAMPIONSHIPS!

We are running the first ever World Championships of MiniGangs! A very beginner friendly game written by Curtis Fell at Ramshackle Games!
But don’t be fooled - it can also be very treacherous even for the more experienced players! But all in all: just tons of fun!
To participate in this tournament you can either bring your own 4 custom minis - or you can borrow a gang that we built and painted for the event!
The rules are literally only two pages and can be downloaded here: ramshacklegames.co.uk

We will adjust the rules slightly for tournament play - but we’ll explain all this when we meet at the fest!
There’s 24 places for the tournament - and you’ll need to sign up on the form below, plus give us 5€ of you hard earned money! (Don’t worry - they’ll be invested straight into the production of some epic boards!)

“OTHER GAMES? YES OF COURSE!”

There will also be a number of other games set up for everyone to play! Forbidden Psalm, Mordheim and The Last War - to name a few! Join our discord server and talk to other players and set up some battles here:
discord.gg/u8D8QaYV

And of course - there shall be kitbashing! Free for all! And you can even enter the competition - where Veronica Levin (@florescentia.grotesque) & Ben Sandrock ( @ben_has_hobbies ) will decide who’s the most crazy of you all!

To finish it all if there will be some live music and DJ’s! We’ll announce the artist a bit later on - but let’s just say we have some goodies for you…

Hope to see you all on the 5th of October in Malmö!

(Since you are here you are probably already following us who organize this thing on instagram - we’ll post all the updates on that platform first!)

S.A.W
